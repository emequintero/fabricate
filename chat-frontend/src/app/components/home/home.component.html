Welcome to Fabricate!

To start chatting, open the sidebar and click the button. Then, add some people to your room and click submit. If all users leave a room, all messages disappear along with the room.

When someone sends you a request to join their room it'll show up under the button in the side bar. Click the button to approve or the button to deny the request.

Each room has controls to add another person or leave the room . To switch between rooms you're in, open the sidebar and click the room.
